Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your goal program.

Can I work on multiple goals?

Yes, but we recommend starting with one goal for best results. Once your first 30-day program becomes automatic, you can layer in a second goal. Trying to do everything at once often leads to doing nothing consistently.

What if I miss a day?

Just pick up where you left off. Consistency matters more than perfection. Missing one day does not reset your progress — what matters is the overall trend.

Are these programs medical treatments?

No. These are habit-building programs focused on general hair and scalp wellness. They do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you have medical concerns about hair loss or scalp conditions, always consult a qualified healthcare professional.

How long until I see results?

Most people notice improvements in hair texture and feel within 2–4 weeks of consistent daily habits. Visible changes in thickness, shine, and resilience typically become more apparent after completing the full 30-day program.
